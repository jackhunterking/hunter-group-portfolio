## Voice Rules

Write in Turkish for a single viewer. Use "sen".

Always write from "biz", "bize", and "bizimle". Do not use singular first person.

Use only these English real estate terms when needed:

- mortgage
- condo
- townhouse
- detached
- closing
- pre-approval
- black mold

Write from the specific video, not the general topic category.

Use asymmetric rhythm. Pair short direct lines with slightly longer explanatory lines.

Avoid hype words and hard CTAs. Do not use:

- DM at
- kaçırma
- hemen tıkla
- bize ulaşın

Generated social copy must not use dashes of any kind. Split sentences, use commas, or use an ellipsis. Hyphens are allowed only in URLs.

Posts should end on their own. Do not force an end question unless the platform file explicitly allows it.
