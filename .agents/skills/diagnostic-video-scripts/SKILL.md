# Diagnostic Video Scripts

## Overview

Generate short-form video ideas that diagnose business growth problems instead of teaching beginner tactics. Every idea must include a hook, concept, full timecoded walkthrough script, and FigJam AI prompt.

## Required Reference

Read `references/framework.md` before generating ideas.

## Output For Every Idea

Always produce all four sections:

1. Topic & Hook: title plus opening hook line for the first 3 seconds.
2. Concept: 2 to 3 sentences naming the symptom, misdiagnosis, real problem, and fix.
3. Full Walkthrough Script: word-for-word with timecodes and bracketed stage directions.
4. FigJam AI Prompt: ready to paste into FigJam.

## Content Rules

Diagnose, do not teach.

Assume the viewer has money and is already spending on ads, agencies, tools, or staff.

Use one idea per video.

Give the WHAT, not the HOW. Name the fix, do not teach step-by-step implementation.

Design for pre-drawn FigJam diagrams on the iPad, not live drawing.

Keep scripts under 90 seconds.

CTA should be authority positioning, such as "I fix this for a living" or "This is what we build for clients". Do not use "follow me for more tips."

## FigJam Prompt Rules

Every FigJam prompt must:

- Say "flowchart" or "diagram".
- List every node or box with its exact label.
- Specify every arrow or connection.
- Call out the problem area with color, dashed border, or "THE GAP" label.
- Include data labels such as "$80/lead" or "1.5% conversion" when used in the script.
- Use one layout direction: left-to-right or top-to-bottom.
- Match labels exactly to the script's words.
- Stay under 200 words.
- End with: "Create only this diagram. Do not add anything else."

If generating a batch, repeat the full four-part output for each idea.
