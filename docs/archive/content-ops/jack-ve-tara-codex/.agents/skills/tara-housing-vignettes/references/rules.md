# Clinical Vignette Rules

## Prohibited Police-Trace Terms

Do not use:

- reported
- report
- filed a report
- called the police
- contacted authorities
- restraining order
- criminal charges
- court order
- 911

Avoid synonyms that imply police or court involvement. Frame escalation through what the woman told Maryam, what Maryam observed, or what neighbors or family noticed.

## Physical Harm Boundary

Allowed with care:

- grabbing
- gripping wrist
- blocking a door
- pushing into furniture
- throwing objects near her
- slamming
- brief pinning
- intimidating gestures

Not allowed:

- choking or strangulation
- weapons
- broken bones
- loss of consciousness
- hospitalization for assault
- detailed sexual assault

Children may be present, witness distress, or be used as leverage. Children are never physically or sexually harmed.

## Variant Rotation

Rotate one of these five structures:

- A: Chronological arc
- B: Themed by abuse type
- C: Incident-anchored
- D: Escalation pattern
- E: Session-anchored

Vary headers, cadence, abuse-profile mix, cultural texture, partner profile, coping details, entry point to Maryam, and symptom clusters.

Mention the variant name in the closing summary after saving the document.
